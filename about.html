About Flameies

At Flameies, we craft more than just candles — we craft experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025, Flameies is a proudly co-led startup by passionate men and women from India. Together, we bring creativity, craftsmanship, and care into every candle we make. Our mission is to deliver premium handcrafted candles with a heart for sustainability.

Why Choose Flameies?

  • 🕯️ 100% natural soy wax
  • 🌿 Eco-friendly packaging
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Handcrafted by skilled artisans
  • 🌸 Long-lasting, delightful aromas
  • ✅ Ethically sourced and cruelty-free

Our Vision

We aim to fill every space with warmth, light, and calm. Flameies envisions a world where conscious living meets beautiful design through the soft glow of a handcrafted flame.
