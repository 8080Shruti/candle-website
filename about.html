About Flameies

At Flameies, we craft more than just candles — we craft experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025, Flameies is a woman-led startup based in India with a passion for artisanal candle making. Our eco-conscious, hand-poured candles are made with love and sustainable ingredients.

Why Flameies?

  • 🕯️ 100% natural soy wax
  • 🌿 Eco-friendly packaging
  • 👩‍🎨 Handcrafted by local artisans
  • 🌸 Long-lasting, delightful aromas
  • ❤️ Ethically sourced and cruelty-free

Our Vision

To bring warmth, wellness, and sustainability to homes across the world with beautifully crafted candles that make a difference.
